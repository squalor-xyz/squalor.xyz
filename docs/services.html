Our Services

At Squalor, LLC, we provide tailored solutions to help your business innovate and succeed.
Below, you’ll find an overview of our core offerings.

Consulting

Our consulting services empower you to tackle complex challenges with confidence. We bring deep expertise to the table, offering strategic guidance in technology and operations. Here’s what we provide:

  • Technology Strategy: We assess your current tech stack, identify gaps, and create a roadmap to align it with your long-term business goals.
  • Organizational Optimization: From team structure to process workflows, we help you streamline operations for maximum efficiency and productivity.
  • Workflow Automation: Specializing in data-heavy industries like semiconductors, we design automated systems to reduce manual effort and improve accuracy.

Technology Development

We craft custom technology solutions that solve your unique problems. Whether it’s software, hardware, or data tools, we deliver precision and innovation. Our services include:

  • RF Test Automation: We develop automated testing systems for integrated circuits (ICs), ensuring reliability and efficiency in RF applications.
  • Custom Software: From web and mobile apps to desktop tools, we build software tailored to your specific needs, with a focus on usability and performance.
  • Hardware Prototyping: Have an idea? We turn it into a working prototype, bridging the gap between concept and reality.
  • Firmware & Embedded Systems: Our team creates robust firmware and embedded solutions for devices requiring high reliability and real-time performance.
  • Data Analysis Tools: We build automated tools to process and analyze data, giving you actionable insights quickly and effectively.

Contracting

  • Ongoing Support: From system maintenance to feature updates, we provide consistent, reliable support to keep your operations running smoothly.
